To turn on, or to turn off, the Service LED on a disk you have to make use of the CellCLI. 
For example we are in need of turning the Sevice LED on for 3 disks we will use the following command:

CellCLI> alter physicaldisk  32:0,32:1,32:2 serviceled on

To disable the Service LED we use the following command:

CellCLI> alter physicaldisk  32:0,32:1,32:2 serviceled off
